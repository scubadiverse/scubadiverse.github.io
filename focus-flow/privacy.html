Privacy Policy – Focus & Flow

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Focus & Flow is a personal focus and wellbeing app. It helps you pace your work, take breaks, drink water, rest your eyes, calm down, and notice small wins. This page explains, in plain language, what information the app handles and how it is kept private.

The short version: everything you enter stays on your own phone. If you choose to create an account, your progress is also backed up to your own private cloud space so you can restore it or use it on another device. Your data is never sold, never shared for advertising, and there are no trackers or ads.

What the app stores

The app records only what you put into it and how you use it, so it can be useful to you:

  • Your projects and daily time targets
  • Focus sessions and the time you spend
  • Feelings / mood check-ins you tap, and any notes you add
  • Small wins, water logs, and your settings (reminders, block length, etc.)
  • If you create an account: your email address (used only to sign you in)

What the app does NOT collect

  • No health-device data. The app does not read your heart rate, stress, steps, or any data from a watch, Health Connect, Apple Health, or similar.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or microphone.
  • No advertising identifiers, no third-party trackers, no ads.

Where your data is stored

  • On your device. By default, everything is saved locally on your phone (in the app's own storage) and never leaves it.
  • In the cloud, only if you sign in. If you create an account, your progress and email are stored in Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Firestore), which acts as our data processor. Security rules ensure only you, signed in to your account, can read or write your data. See Firebase's privacy information.

Why we store it

Only to make the app work for you: to show your progress, remind you, and back it up so you don't lose it. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone for advertising or marketing. The only third party involved is Google Firebase, which stores the data on our behalf so the app can sync.

Keeping and deleting your data

  • On device: clearing the app's data or uninstalling it removes the local copy.
  • Account / cloud: you can sign out at any time. To permanently delete your account and all cloud data, email us (below) and we will delete it, or use the in-app delete option where available.

Children

This app is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. Please do not create an account if you are under the age required in your country.

Not medical advice

Focus & Flow offers general wellbeing support (breathing, breaks, encouragement). It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional help. If you are struggling, please reach out to someone you trust or a qualified professional.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
