How ProjectSavvy works

A calm way to manage several projects, your time, and yourself. Here's the quick tour.

📁 Add your projects

Tap Edit next to Projects to rename them, set a daily time target, or give each one its own colour. You can have up to 7.

The colours you pick show up on your charts, so each project is easy to spot.

▶️ Start a focus block

Pick a project, choose how long you want to focus, and tap Start. Work in one kind, steady block at a time instead of trying to do everything at once.

🤔 Not sure which project?

Tap Help me choose. ProjectSavvy suggests which project to give time to next, so nothing gets forgotten and every project keeps moving.

💧 Breaks that look after you

The app reminds you to drink water, rest your eyes, and stand up. After a long stretch online it gives you a real 2-minute time-out so you actually step away from the screen.

During a time-out you can still reach your emergency contacts at any time.

💙 How do you feel?

Tap how you feel and the app meets you where you are: box breathing, a simple grounding exercise, and calming rain or white-noise sounds when things feel like too much.

⭐ Small wins

Notice the little things you did today and log them. They add up, and a gentle nudge reminds you that slow progress is still progress.

📈 Your charts & rhythm

See your time per project, your pace, and a day-by-day trend. Over time ProjectSavvy learns your strongest hours and when you tend to tire.

🔒 Private by design

Everything stays on your phone. No account, no sign-up, no ads, no trackers, and your data is never sold or shared.
