🌿 Thoughtsieve

A calm space to relieve stress: unload a worry, see the thinking trap behind it, and let it go – then, in your own words, turn it into something kinder. Uses simple CBT ideas.

🔒 Private. Everything you write stays on this device only – nothing is ever uploaded or shared.
